Supplying Freshness and Value Through Wholesale Organic Produce

At Pitco Foods, we’ve always believed that the quality of the ingredients we provide is a direct reflection of the partnerships we build. As a trusted name in food distribution, we supply a wide range of businesses with products they can rely on—and our commitment to wholesale organic produce is a big part of that promise. From restaurants and grocery stores to foodservice operations, we help our customers meet the growing demand for organic fruits and vegetables while maintaining the consistency and affordability their businesses need.

Our years in this industry have taught us that experience, consistency, and care matter just as much as the products themselves. That’s why we focus on providing not just fresh produce, but long-term support and service that helps our customers thrive.

Why Businesses Choose Wholesale Organic Produce

In today’s food industry, sourcing organic isn’t a passing trend—it’s a smart business decision that aligns with consumer values. Choosing wholesale means businesses can meet that demand without sacrificing efficiency or stretching budgets.
There are several important reasons our customers turn to us when they’re sourcing organic at scale:

Consistent Freshness

We’ve built strong, long-standing relationships with certified organic growers across the region. These partnerships allow us to receive shipments of produce shortly after harvest, meaning the fruits and vegetables we deliver to our customers are as fresh as possible. This freshness doesn’t just impact taste—it also plays a role in shelf life, presentation, and customer satisfaction.

Smart Pricing

Organic produce can be more costly than conventional alternatives at the retail level. But through wholesale sourcing, those costs are significantly reduced. We use our buying power and direct relationships with farms to negotiate competitive rates, helping our customers stay profitable while offering high-quality organic options.

Access to Variety

From everyday staples like carrots and spinach to specialty items like heirloom tomatoes or watermelon radishes, our selection is always growing. As demand for variety continues to increase, we keep our product lists updated and reflective of what’s in season and what consumers are seeking.

Our Quality-First Approach

At Pitco Foods, we don’t compromise on standards. Every piece of produce that passes through our distribution centers is subject to rigorous quality control processes. We examine for freshness, cleanliness, and structural integrity before anything gets packed or loaded for delivery.

We’ve been in this business for years, and we know that poor-quality produce hurts not just your operations, but also your customer relationships. That’s why we prioritize attention to detail at every step. If it’s not something we’d be proud to serve ourselves, it doesn’t make it to your door.

In addition to our in-house standards, all our organic products carry proper certifications. We only work with growers who follow USDA organic practices and can provide documentation on their methods, inspections, and supply chain.

Serving a Wide Range of Customers

We work with businesses of all sizes and formats, and that flexibility is a core part of our strength. Whether you run a family-owned corner market or manage multiple restaurant locations, our team is here to help you find the right mix of produce to suit your needs.

For Restaurants and Cafés

Chefs and kitchen managers often require both flexibility and consistency. Our ability to deliver fresh organic produce on a regular schedule—even with fluctuating menus and changing order sizes—makes us a reliable partner. We’re used to handling last-minute requests and providing guidance on what’s currently in season or at its best quality.

For Grocery Stores and Retail Markets

Presentation is key when it comes to retail produce. We provide well-packaged, display-ready organic items that hold up on the shelf and attract customer attention. From bulk orders to carefully curated assortments, we help our retail partners build displays that reflect quality and freshness.

For Schools, Hospitals, and Institutions

Organic choices are increasingly common in institutional foodservice, and we’re proud to support that shift. Our team ensures that the product meets safety, certification, and documentation requirements for public and private institutions alike.

A Transparent and Trust-Based Supply Chain

Our customers count on us to be more than just a distributor—they count on us to be a reliable partner they can trust. That’s why we keep our supply chain transparent and our communication open. Every batch of produce is traceable to its source, with certification paperwork and inspection records available upon request.

We also maintain clear communication about product availability and seasonality. If a certain item is at the end of its growing cycle or is affected by weather, we’re upfront about it and ready with alternatives or suggestions. That honesty has helped us build long-lasting partnerships rooted in mutual respect and shared goals.

Sustainability and Responsible Sourcing

Our support of organic farming isn’t just about meeting demand—it’s also about making choices that support healthy ecosystems and long-term agricultural sustainability.

By sourcing from organic farms that use crop rotation, natural compost, and water-efficient irrigation methods, we help reduce the environmental impact of food production. We know our customers care about these issues too, and we’re proud to be part of a supply chain that values environmental responsibility.

Additionally, we regularly assess our packaging and delivery practices to ensure that our operations are efficient and low-impact. From reusable crates to route planning that minimizes transportation emissions, we’re always working to do better—for our customers and for the planet.

Seamless Logistics and Delivery

Delivering perishable goods requires precision and care. That’s why our logistics systems are built to ensure that organic produce arrives on time, in top condition. We use refrigerated transport, temperature-controlled storage, and careful handling procedures to preserve freshness throughout the journey from farm to store.

Our distribution centers are strategically located to serve Northern and Southern California, with a fleet that’s ready to support businesses of all types. Whether you need daily, weekly, or custom delivery schedules, we’ll work with you to make it happen.

Need to adjust your order? Have a question about an upcoming shipment? Our customer service team is trained, responsive, and ready to help. That’s the kind of service we believe every business deserves.

Supporting Our Customers' Success

Our work doesn’t end at delivery. We’re here to help our customers grow by offering resources, product knowledge, and responsive support. Want advice on seasonal trends? Need help building an organic produce section that drives traffic? Just ask. Our team is experienced and always willing to help you make informed, confident decisions.

Because we serve such a wide variety of businesses, we’ve seen a lot of what works—and what doesn’t. That means we can offer insights based on real-world results, not just theory. And we’re always happy to share what we’ve learned to support our customers’ continued growth.

Looking Ahead

The demand for organic produce is only growing. As more consumers prioritize food quality, sustainability, and transparency, businesses that meet those needs will continue to stand out. At Pitco Foods, we’re committed to staying ahead of those trends—not by following fads, but by staying grounded in what’s always mattered: quality, trust, and service.

We’re constantly expanding our supplier relationships, improving our internal systems, and working closely with customers to adapt to the future of food. When you work with us, you’re not just getting a supplier—you’re getting a team that’s in it with you for the long haul.

Conclusion

At Pitco Foods, our dedication to freshness, consistency, and reliability sets us apart as a trusted partner in the world of wholesale organic produce. We’re proud to help businesses across California bring high-quality organic fruits and vegetables to their customers—day in and day out. If you’re looking for a partner who understands your needs and shares your values, we’d be honored to work with you.
